Origin Story

At the ripe age of 3 years old, Der Kront was abandoned by his parents to become an orphan who lived in the streets living with the homeless, stealing from houses, killed a man and went to war for Glattonsburg where he was traumatized by the effects that war had on him from seeing the things he had to see. In spite of this, one day he found this to be a romance he lusted that came from nowhere. So he turns out to be a no good bastard who wants to hold power of the city, a devilish villainous viking who shits on the poverty, falls in the place of his own free will he reigns supreme over the tyranny he creates making life for others hell. He is not in full power but he is in the process of evil demolition for order that he wants. He turns on who he was and through his life experience, it altered his chemical brain balance to become who he is through tragedy.
